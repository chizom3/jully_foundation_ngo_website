Program Focus

What this program aims to achieve

Reusable Pad Production addresses period poverty, school absenteeism, health education, and women's enterprise at the same time. The program trains women and girls to understand menstrual hygiene while creating practical reusable products.

Key outcomes

  • check_circle Improve menstrual dignity for girls and women in underserved communities.
  • check_circle Reduce school absenteeism linked to lack of sanitary products.
  • check_circle Train women to produce reusable pads safely and consistently.
  • check_circle Create small income opportunities through local production and distribution.

How we work

Participants learn menstrual health, fabric selection, sewing, hygiene standards, packaging, and community education. The program can also support schools and IDP communities through distribution and awareness sessions.
