Who We Are

Empowering Communities, Inspiring Progress.

We bridge the gap between grassroots African community action and international NGO standards, working tirelessly to foster sustainable development and human-centric progress.

Our Story

Jully Foundation is a women- and youth-led nonprofit organization founded by Osueke Chiamaka Juliet to advance climate action, education, women's economic empowerment, peacebuilding, and social inclusion in underserved communities.

The Foundation works with women, girls, youth, persons with disabilities, and internally displaced persons, especially those affected by flooding, poverty, unemployment, conflict, and environmental degradation.

Our work turns community challenges into practical opportunities by combining green vocational skills, circular economy training, mental wellness support, entrepreneurship, and civic empowerment.

Our Philosophy

Dignity, structure, and measurable community progress.

Jully Foundation believes sustainable development must be done with communities, not simply for them. Our work begins with listening, then builds practical systems that help local people lead, sustain, and measure change.
