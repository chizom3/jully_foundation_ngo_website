Strategic Plan

Jully Foundation's strategic direction centers on climate action, circular economy enterprise, girls' education, reusable pad production, mental wellness, civic empowerment, peacebuilding, and social inclusion for vulnerable communities.

Skills to Income

Connect training to mentorship, microgrants, zero-interest loan opportunities, and market access.

Waste to Value

Turn textile and plastic waste into reusable pads, bags, artworks, and household products.

Inclusion and Peace

Support women, girls, youth, PWDs, IDPs, and flood-affected communities through dignity-first programs.
