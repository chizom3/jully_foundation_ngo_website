Program Focus

What this program aims to achieve

Civic Engagement and Peacebuilding helps communities participate peacefully and responsibly in public life. It encourages dialogue, inclusion, accountability, and local leadership among women, youth, persons with disabilities, and other vulnerable groups.

Key outcomes

  • check_circle Increase awareness of civic rights, duties, and peaceful participation.
  • check_circle Promote dialogue between citizens, community leaders, and local institutions.
  • check_circle Strengthen women and youth voices in community decision-making.
  • check_circle Reduce conflict by encouraging communication, tolerance, and shared problem solving.

How we work

The program uses town-hall style conversations, leadership training, peace circles, voter education, and community forums. Activities are designed to help people move from silence or frustration into constructive participation.
