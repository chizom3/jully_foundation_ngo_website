Program Focus

What this program aims to achieve

Women's Economic Empowerment supports women to move from survival income to more stable and dignified livelihoods. It focuses on practical business knowledge, confidence, market access, and support systems that help women grow.

Key outcomes

  • check_circle Build women's skills in enterprise planning, pricing, savings, and customer care.
  • check_circle Support women-led microbusinesses with mentorship and cooperative learning.
  • check_circle Link participants to microgrants, zero-interest loans, and market opportunities where possible.
  • check_circle Increase women's decision-making power within households and communities.

How we work

The program combines business training, peer groups, mentoring, product development, and follow-up support. It is designed to help women turn skills into income that can last beyond the training period.
