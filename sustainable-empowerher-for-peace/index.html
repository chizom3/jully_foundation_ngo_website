Program Focus

What this program aims to achieve

Sustainable EmpowerHer for Peace is designed for women and girls who need more than short-term relief. It combines practical livelihood training with leadership development, peace education, and community healing so participants can become active builders of stability in their homes and communities.

Key outcomes

  • check_circle Strengthen women's confidence, voice, and participation in local decision-making.
  • check_circle Support women to build income from green and creative vocational skills.
  • check_circle Promote peaceful dialogue, tolerance, and community problem solving.
  • check_circle Connect trained women to mentorship, markets, and small enterprise opportunities.

How we work

The program uses hands-on sessions, peer groups, mentoring circles, and community dialogue. Participants learn by doing, then receive follow-up support that helps them convert new skills into practical action.
